About The Observer

We are here to deliver the latest news or events that the Philippines have experienced through the use of flash cards.

Our Mission

The Observer is committed to delivering timely Philippines news through innovative flash card presentation. We focus on exposing corruption and promoting transparency in government operations.

Educational approach to current events and corruption reporting

Simplified news delivery for diverse learning environments

Bootcamp-friendly approach to investigative journalism

Our Values

These core principles guide our work in delivering accessible and educational journalism.

Transparency

We believe in open, honest reporting that makes complex corruption cases accessible to everyone through clear, visual storytelling.

Education Focus

Our educational approach helps students and the community understand important issues through engaging, easy-to-digest content.

Innovation

We use modern flash card techniques and visual design to make journalism more engaging and memorable for our audience.
